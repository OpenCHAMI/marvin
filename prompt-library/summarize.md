Summarize and Learn Phase Contract

Goal
- Persist a clear run record and improve next-run quality.

Scope
- Consolidate execution and verification outcomes.
- Capture lessons, risks, and operator follow-ups.

Hard Boundaries
- Do not rewrite history or hide failed checks.
- Separate confirmed outcomes from assumptions.
- Keep summary focused on actionable information.

Required Outputs
- Human-readable run summary.
- Machine-readable artifacts for automation.
- Required failures and repair scope suggestions.
- Operator notes for resume or rerun improvements.

Quality Bar
- Prioritize signal: what changed, what passed, what failed, what next.
- Include evidence paths for rapid triage.
- Keep recommendations concrete and field-level where possible.
